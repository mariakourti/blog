Technologies In The Video Game Industry

May 07, 2023 — John Smith
The video game industry has been drastically growing the recent years. Its' technology always finds new ways to evolve and improve. Technologies such as: Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR) and Mixed Reality (MR) are some of the most common ones used. Furthermore, more advanced ones can be found. Cloud based technology, detailed graphics, speech and face recognition, AI, and many more, get better by the day.
